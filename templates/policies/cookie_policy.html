Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how example.com ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. Our cookies are used exclusively for authentication/authorization purposes and to collect anonymous statistics about user interactions with our website to help us improve our services.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by your web browser. They allow us to recognize your device, manage your session, and gather anonymous usage data.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Authentication and Authorization: We use cookies to manage user sessions, keep you logged in securely, and ensure that only authorized users can access certain features of our website.
  • Usage Statistics: We may use cookies to collect anonymous data regarding how users interact with our website. This data helps us understand usage patterns, improve functionality, and enhance the overall user experience.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to disable or delete cookies; however, please note that doing so may affect the functionality of our website, particularly features that require secure authentication and user sessions.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
